Welcome to our FAQs!

  • How do I register in the website?


    • Go to the registration page, you will see the fields required to type your information, give those information as the field requires you to then click on "submit".


  • How do they validate the university-related email system?

    • This is automated system, designed by a script that allows the system to check the email by comparing to the university's database to vertify the account email.


  • How do I contact anyone if I have any technical issue?

    • Hover your mouse pointer to the "Contact Us" link in the navigational bar then fill out the fields as instructed to and click on submit.


  • How I do use the search directory page?

    • Hover your mouse pointer to the "Directory" link then fill out the fields as instructed to. After doing that, you can click on any one of two checkbox for student or mentor and click on submit. The automated script will do most of job by searching your desired information to match in database to print results in box below the page.


  • There are any links to other universities?

    • In the home page, there is numerous links to other universities.
